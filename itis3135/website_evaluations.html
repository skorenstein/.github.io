Website Evaluations

Cameron's World

Cameron's world is a web collage made up of images and text which were found archived in GeoCities pages between (1994-2009)

  • Contrast - While the contrast in some areas of the website is good other parts blend together and are hard to read.
  • Repetition - Each section uses different formatting including font, text size, and image size making it difficult to transition from one section to another
  • Alignment - THe alignment of text and images is all over the place including but not limited to the corders around images and the lack of straight edges.
  • Proximity - While the sections are generally organized under a theme there is almost no blank space which amkes it extremley hard to read

OMMO

OMMO is a webflow development partner and won a CSS award for their website design

  • Contrast - The contrast between text, background, and images leads to an easy to read but visually distinct design that is easy on the eyes.
  • Repetition - Each section is distinct but there is a clear pattern for the background and the text.
  • Alignment - Everything is aligned in the same way with straight edges on the margins as well as cooresponding things going together.
  • Proximity - Blank space allows for easy readability and reduces cluttering.
